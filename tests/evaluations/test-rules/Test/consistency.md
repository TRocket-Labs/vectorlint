Check for terminology consistency across the document:

- Flag when the same concept is referred to by different terms (e.g. "user" vs "customer" vs "client")
- Flag inconsistent capitalization of product or feature names
- Flag mixed British and American spelling (e.g. "colour" and "color" used in the same document)
