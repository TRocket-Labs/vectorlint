# Passive Voice

Flag any sentence that uses passive voice in a way that makes the writing less clear, less direct, or more evasive — for example, when the doer is omitted and that omission reduces accountability or clarity ("Mistakes were made"), or when a modal passive weakens an instruction ("The file should be saved before closing"). 

Do not flag passive voice when the sentence is describing a state, condition, or feature rather than an action; when the doer is obvious, irrelevant, or already established in the immediately preceding sentence; or when an active rewrite would be awkward or less natural than the original.
